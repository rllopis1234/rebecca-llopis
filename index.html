About

Rebecca
Llopis

Born in Geneva, raised between Cannes and London, and now based in Los Angeles — where I'm pursuing an undergraduate degree at USC.

I am an actor at heart, a creative thinker, entrepreneur, and innovator. My work sits at the intersection of performance, production, and philosophy — driven by a belief that the most powerful stories are those brave enough to explore the unpredictable, multifaceted nature of being human.

From directing theatre productions in London to producing short films at USC's School of Cinematic Arts, to writing and photographing in my own time — I move fluidly across disciplines because I believe the best work emerges from artists who refuse to be contained by a single form.

I'm currently seeking deeper exposure on the business and production side of film, connecting artists across industries and developing projects that demand to be told.
